About Audio Loop Creator

Audio Loop Creator is your ultimate online tool for music production. Designed for beginners and pros alike, it allows you to:

  • Create seamless audio loops quickly.
  • Export loops as high-quality WAV files.
  • Collaborate with other musicians worldwide.
  • Access a growing library of sound packs and samples.


Key Features

Our platform offers a range of features to enhance your music creation experience:


Intuitive Editor

Trim, loop, and mix audio in a few clicks with our simple, powerful editor.

High Quality

Preserve the original quality of your recordings with lossless export options.

Community Sharing

Showcase your loops, get feedback, and discover creative ideas from other users.

Cloud Storage

Save your loops in the cloud and access them anywhere, anytime.
